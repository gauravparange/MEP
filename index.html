About Us

Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing Services (MEPS) has been a leader in the field since 2015. Previously working on smaller projects, the company has now become one of India's most respected organizations in the areas of plumbing, sanitary systems, fire fighting, pumps, and treatment plants. Our service portfolio extends across the country, catering to Hotels, Commercial complexes, Residential buildings, Malls, and Multiplexes. We have earned a reputation for delivering high-quality projects on time.

MEPS is backed by a highly skilled and technical team that oversees projects on a day-to-day basis, ensuring the highest standards of safety, quality, and adherence to time frames.
